What is serve2perform®?
A talent and leadership development innovation that leverages service to provide unlimited opportunities worldwide to learn, teach, grow, and make a difference.
Why serve2perform®?
A talent and leadership development innovation that leverages service to provide unlimited opportunities worldwide to learn, teach, grow, and make a difference.
Perpetual Learning and Development
  • This innovation is scalable as it is a learning and development mindset and not a finite learning and development program.
  • It’s a more respaonsive strategy to provide just-in-time learning and development opportunities in today’s ever-changing workplace and marketplace, because individuals can engage the innovation as the need arises.
  • serve2perform® helps organizations overcome the issue of limited availability and accessibility to their training programs, which is created by the cost associated with delivering traditional learning and development programs.
  • It improves the effectiveness of training programs by empowering individuals to engage readily available service engagements to apply and adopt the knowledge gained.
  • This innovation perpetuates peer-to-peer learning, which transfers valuable tacit knowledge that only comes from years of experience working in a specific environment or performing a specific role and/or task.

Performance Management
  • serve2perform® helps organizations engage the diversity of their workforce and facilitate connections on a regular basis to mobilize their diversity of thought to drive innovation.
  • This innovation affords the millennial generation, who tend to be more entrepreneurial-minded, to act on their desire to contribute to thought leadership and game-changing transformation.
  • It helps develop high performing individuals by helping them acquire connections, resources, and experiences.
  • serve2perform® helps address a major challenge of many organizations, which is not having a clear strategy on how to mitigate the effects of lost knowledge caused by the continuous retirement of baby boomers. This innovation facilitates the transfer of valuable institutional knowledge to subsequent Generations.
  • It provides organizations with a strategy for mobilizing reverse mentoring (Millennials mentoring Baby Boomers) to help baby boomers in the workplace overcome their skills gap caused by the significant influence of technology.
Retention and Engagement
  • serve2perform® helps develop individuals with deeper roots through the connections and investments made to their organization and community, which in turn, makes them more likely to be retained than their peers who are not.
  • By design, the serve2perform® innovation does not only grow the engagement of individuals in their communities, it also grows and sustains peer-to-peer learning, teaching, and resourcing in the workplace.
  • This innovation helps engage the many employees worldwide who are disengaged at work. Studies show that disengaged employees are also unproductive employees.
Reputation and Brand Management
  • serve2perform® addresses a key requirement of the largest generation of today’s workforce, the Millennial Generation. This generation highly desire the opportunity to serve causes of interest and to make a difference in their community.
  • This innovation helps organizations strengthen their social responsibility, which is critical as many consumers, investors, and potential employees, are prioritizing social responsibility as an essential criterion when deciding which organizations to endorse.
  • By strengthening social responsibility, serve2perform® helps organizations generate social and reputation capital, which has direct implications to operational performance and critical stakeholder relations.
Community Vitality
  • Community-based organizations are the backbone of communities worldwide and many are struggling to survive due to a lack of succession and sustainability planning, and consequently, are in dire need of business consulting that is cost prohibitive to them. serve2perform® facilitates valuable and readily available pro bono consulting.
  • serve2perform® helps sustain community-based organizations which are responsible for affecting the quality of life essential for organizations to recruit, retain, and grow talent and ultimately compete in the global marketplace.
  • Business and community-based organizations succeed and win when they purposely seek out and invest in each other’s challenges and opportunities. serve2perform® builds long lasting partnerships that optimize collective resources and maximize collective impact.
