Meet the Team
Dr. Adam Arroyos
Founder & CEO
As Chief Executive Officer, Dr. Adam Arroyos is responsible for the continued growth and innovation of the company. Adam has an expertise in performance management, thought leadership, organizational development, diversity and inclusion, stakeholder engagement, and executive development. He has held leadership positions at Walmart Stores, Inc., SVI, JCPenney, Northwest Arkansas Community College, and ALPFA. Dr. Arroyos has been recognized as one of the Top 100 Most Influential Executives in Corporate America by Hispanic Business, he has been a featured guest on CNN’s Latino en America, and was recently appointed by the Governor of Arkansas to the State Career Education and Workforce Development Board.
Pete Leer
Chief Administrative Officer
As Chief Administrative Officer, Pete is responsible for the day-to-day operations of GPA including human resources, financial management, contract negotiations and pricing, strategic planning, and project management. Prior to joining GPA, Pete had a 35 year career as a hospital administrator in both acute care hospitals and outpatient clinics located in northwest Arkansas and south central Missouri.
Tamara Williams
Senior Associate
As Senior Associate, Tamara Williams is responsible for business development, relationship management, project management, instructional design, program facilitation, coaching and communications. Tamara has diverse expertise in strategic communications, training and development, project management, IT support, content development and marketing. She has held leadership positions at Wal-Mart Stores, Inc., Sprint and EMBARQ; she also owned and operated Right At Your Service, an IT consulting firm. Ms. Williams is a catalyst for inclusion and engagement, as well as an advocate for mentoring and giving back to the community.
